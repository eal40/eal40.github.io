Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 1, 2024

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Eugene Alforte ("I", "me", or "my") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit my portfolio website at eugenealforte.github.io (the "Website").

Information I Collect

Analytics Data

I use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with my website. This service collects:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • General location (country/city level)
  • Device and browser information
  • How you arrived at my website (referrer)
  • Anonymous usage patterns and interactions

Contact Form Data

When you submit the contact form, I collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • The message you send
  • Timestamp of submission

Download Tracking

I track when visitors download my resume or project installers to understand interest in my work. This includes:

  • Which files are downloaded
  • Download timestamps
  • General usage statistics

How I Use Information

I use collected information to:

  • Understand how visitors use my website
  • Improve the website's content and functionality
  • Respond to contact form submissions
  • Track the effectiveness of my portfolio
  • Identify technical issues and improve performance

Data Sharing

I do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • Google Analytics: Anonymous usage data is processed by Google under their privacy policy
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect my rights

Cookies and Tracking

This website uses cookies for:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics cookies to track website usage
  • Preferences: Remembering your consent choices
  • Functionality: Ensuring the website works properly

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of analytics tracking
  • Request information about data I have collected
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact me using the information below.

Data Security

I implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure hosting with regular security updates
  • Limited data collection and retention
  • Regular security monitoring

Data Retention

I retain data for the following periods:

  • Analytics Data: 26 months (Google Analytics default)
  • Contact Form Data: Until responded to, then archived for 1 year
  • Download Logs: 12 months for statistical purposes

Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics (Privacy Policy)
  • GitHub Pages: Website hosting (Privacy Policy)
  • Font Awesome: Icons via CDN (Privacy Policy)

Changes to This Policy

I may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to exercise your rights, please contact me:

  • Email: ealforte214@gmail.com
  • Website: Contact Form

Opt-Out Options

You can opt out of tracking:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Do Not Track: Enable "Do Not Track" in your browser settings
  • Cookie Settings: Use your browser's cookie management tools
